Brand strategy, visual identity, product design

Strategic clarity before committing to creative work.

Built for mid-sized companies. Eight stages. One document. BRIEF is the strategy foundation: category forces, competitor mapping, and strategic synthesis delivered as a brief you can act on immediately — independently, or as the starting point for everything that follows.

Works for: New brand Brand refresh Brand extension Product sub-brand
01
Category Intake
Mode, market, geography, logistics constraints. The brief that shapes everything that follows.
02
Competitor Discovery
Four-source search: review sites, analyst coverage, practitioner communities, recent entrants.
03
Brand Ecosystem Map
Actor categories, relationships, power concentrations. Who controls access, price, and credibility.
04
PESTEL Analysis
30–40 scored forces across 6 dimensions, sourced and tagged for regulatory state.
05
Cross-Map Matrix
Forces mapped against ecosystem actors: exposure, opportunity, asymmetric dynamics.
06
Strategic Themes
4–6 named tensions that define the industry moment and the brand's entry point into it.
07
Brand Strategy Inputs
Positioning tensions, differentiation levers, narrative anchors, positioning statement, competitor map.
08
Brand Identity Inputs
Brand core, archetype, brand promise, tone of voice. The character layer that makes the strategy visible.
